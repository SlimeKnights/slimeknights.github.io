Battlesign

The battlesign is a combination of a melee weapon and a shield. It will typically be located in bastions, and can be obtained from piglins.

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • Medium Block Potential
  • 3 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot

Swasher

The swasher is a combination of a melee weapon and a handheld fluid pistol found in various ocean locations.

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • High Attack Speed
  • Fluid Pistol
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
