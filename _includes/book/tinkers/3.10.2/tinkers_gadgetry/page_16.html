Melting Pan

The melting pan will melt your blocks and hold fluid for your staffly goals. It can be found from dungeons, igloos, husks, and as a gift from villagers.

Properties:

  • Mines meltable blocks
  • Melting+
  • 1 Defense Slot
  • 1 Upgrade Slot
  • 2 Ability Slots

War Pick

The war pick is a combination of pickaxe and a crossbow. It can be found in mineshafts and obtained from villagers or illagers.

Properties:

  • Mines stone blocks and similar
  • Able to keep an arrow prepared to fire
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
