Slimeshell

Slimeshells are one part defense, one part storage, granting the best of both shulker abilities.

To create these wondrous pants, pour enderslime on a shulker shell in a basin. The shell can later be repaired using chorus.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • No Armor or Defense Slots
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Shulking

Slimeboots

These boots combine the power of the rabbit with slime to allow you to jump around with ease.

To create these fantastic boots, pour enderslime on a rabbits foot. The boots can later be repaired using rabbit hide.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • No Armor or Defense Slots
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Bouncy and Leaping
