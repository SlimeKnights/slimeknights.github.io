Earthslime Staff

Using the power of green slime, you can make the most defensive of magic staffs. Has higher block potential and accuracy, but worse projectile power.

Properties:

  • Mid Durability
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 3 Defense Slots
  • 2 Ability Slots

Ichor Staff

Ichor is uncommon stuff, but allows making some of the strongest staffs. Grants higher velocity at the cost of drawspeed.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 3 Ability Slots
