Ichor Geodes

Ichor geodes are one of the best sources of the rare orange slime. They appear at high altitudes in the nether, and the crystals appear to glow as well.

Enderslime Geodes

Enderslime geodes can be found around the outer islands in the end, both underground and floating on their own. Sometimes it's hard to tell a geode apart from the other rocks floating in that sky.
