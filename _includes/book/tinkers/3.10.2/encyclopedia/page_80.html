Gilded

Trades 1 ability slot for 2 upgrade slots.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Lucky

Increases drop rates from both blocks (harvest tools only) and monsters. Luck requires a different recipe for each level, applied in order.

Leggings can receive a single level of luck, which boosts drops from mobs and drops from blocks when using tinker tools.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • First level requires 1 ability slot
  • Second and third levels are slotless
