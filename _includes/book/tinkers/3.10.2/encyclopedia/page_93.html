Exchanging

Replaces mined blocks with the block held in the offhand.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Silky

Causes mined blocks to drop themselves unmodified. For swords, cleavers, scythes, and daggers, additionally allows shearing mobs.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
