Molten Uranium

Radioactive decay from molten uranium isn't safe to be around, so dump it on your enemies.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 10 seconds Poison

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Poison

Molten Cobalt

Inhaling the lingering cloud is the safest way to use molten cobalt for haste, or just use it as a weapon.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • 7 seconds Haste

  • 7 seconds Speed

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Haste and 7 seconds Speed
