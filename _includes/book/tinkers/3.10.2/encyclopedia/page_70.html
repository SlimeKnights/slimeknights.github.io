Nearsighted

Increases the FOV by 5% per level while wearing this armor or holding this tool.

This modifier is ideal to cancel out the FOV change from the heavy tools, but you may find other uses for it.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Offhanded

Prevents using the main hand's interaction ability if the offhand is occupied. For example, an axe will not strip wood if a shield is in the offhand.

The second level entirely disables the tool interaction ability.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
