Cooling

Grants +1.6 damage per level against mobs immune to fire.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Killager

Grants +2 damage per level against villagers and illagers.

An villager or illager is anything that has a squidward nose.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
