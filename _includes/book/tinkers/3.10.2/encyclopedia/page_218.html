Molten Netherite

Molten netherite is the highest base damage, for when you cannot wait for your target to bleed out.

Entity Effects:

  • 5.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Blindness

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Blindness

Molten Enderium

Molten enderium is ideal as a weapon, keeping enemies from teleporting as you fight.

Entity Effects:

  • 4.0 Magic Damage

  • 10 seconds Enderference

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Enderference
