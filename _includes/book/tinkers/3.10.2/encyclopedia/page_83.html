Brushing

Hold interact on a suspicious block to slowly brush it. Consumes durability upon completion and supports AOE brushing.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Bucketing

Allows the tool to pick up fluids on use and place fluids when sneaking. Also increases the tools tank capacity by 1000 mb.

Tools with bucketing have the ability to be filled and drained by interacting with fluid tank blocks.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
