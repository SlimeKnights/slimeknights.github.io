Tool Belt

Allows swapping the first 4 hotbar slots using the leggings interact. Each consecutive level adds an additional slot until reaching the max 9 slots at level 6.

Effects:

  • First level requires 1 ability slot
  • Max 6 levels
  • Different recipe each level

Bouncy

Causes you to bounce upon landing, preventing fall damage while applying 90% of your velocity upwards.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
