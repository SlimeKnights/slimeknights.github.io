Springy

Has a 15% chance per level to apply up 2 to 4 blocks of knockback per level to the attacker. Applies effect from the piece with the largest bonus.

Shields are twice as effective but only work when blocking.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Thorns

Has a 15% chance per level to apply 1 to 4 damage to the attacker. Multiple pieces will stack the effect.

Shields are twice as effective but only work when blocking.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
