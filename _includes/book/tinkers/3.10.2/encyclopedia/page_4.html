Dagger

The Dagger is a quick precise weapon, usable in both hands. Identical daggers can be stacked to 2 when modifying they are undamaged, providing a discount on modifiers.

Silky grants shearing.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 65% Attack Modifier
  • 2.0 Attack Speed
  • 75% Durability Multiplier
  • Padded I
  • Offhand attack

Flint and Brick

Flint and Brick is a handheld tool starting with firestarter.

Expanded increases the radius of fire, which is further increased by fireprimer.

Properties:

  • +0 Attack Damage
  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 100 Durability
  • Fiery 1
  • Firestarting
