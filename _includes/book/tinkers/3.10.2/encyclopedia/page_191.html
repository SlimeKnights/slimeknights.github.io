Powdered Snow

Powdered snow is a useful fluid for nether combat, dealing bonus damage to blazing mobs.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Cold Damage

  • +4 seconds freeze

Block Effects:

  • Places Snow

Potion

Potions allow a wide variety of effects crafted in a brewing stand. Can dump potions into tanks to fill tools.

Entity Effects:

  • Applies effects from potion

Block Effects:

  • Lingering potion cloud
