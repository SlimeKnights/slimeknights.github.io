Stripping

Allows the tool to strip wood, similarly to hand or broad axes. Tools will use their normal mining area for stripping wood.

Triggers before the tool's native tilling or pathing if present.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Tilling

Allows the tool to till dirt, similarly to mattocks or scythes. Tools will use their normal mining area for tilling.

Triggers before the tool's native pathing or stripping if present.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
