Protection Defense Modifiers

Protection defense modifiers increase resistance to a particular type of damage. All damage sources should fit into one of the five types, and types can be freely mixed on armor and shields for passive benefits.

Blast Protection

Grants +10% resistance against explosion damage, such as from creepers or TNT. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 8 levels.

Reduces the knockback from explosions by 15% per level of the highest level piece.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
