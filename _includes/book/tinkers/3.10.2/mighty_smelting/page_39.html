Sledge Hammer

The Sledge Hammer is a broad mining tool. It harvests blocks in a wide range and is effective against undead.

It is optimal for mining out large areas and long mining trips.

Properties:

  • Advanced mining tool
  • 3x3 AoE Mining
  • Effective on stone and ores
  • Starts with Smite II

Vein Hammer

The vein hammer is a broad mining tool. It harvests blocks of the same type in veins.

It is optimal for getting all ores in a vein in one hit.

Properties:

  • Advanced mining tool
  • Vein mining
  • Effective on stone and ores
  • Bonus piercing damage
