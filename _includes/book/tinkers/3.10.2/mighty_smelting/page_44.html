Melting

Smeltery not portable enough? Try turning your tool into a handheld smeltery!

Right clicking a tank while holding the tool to empty fluid.

Effects:

  • Melts attacked mobs into the tool's tank
  • Dropped items also are melted if possible
  • Tool can be emptied into a tank or a smeltery drain
  • Requires 1 ability slot, single level

Unbreakable

Broken things are not fun, thankfully things can be made unbreakable.

Effects:

  • Your tool is now unbreakable
  • Single Level
  • Requires 1 ability slot, Reinforced 5, Netherite
  • Not effective if you try to eat your tool
