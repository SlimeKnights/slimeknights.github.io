General Abilities

Abilities are a special type of modifier that can drastically alter how your tool performs. These require an ability slot specifically, which are rarer than upgrade slots, but stronger per slot.

These abilities will enhance your tool all around!

Expanded

Bigger is always better right?

Effects:

  • Increases mining width & height
  • Range is different on every tool
  • Multiple levels, each level increases width & height by one
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
