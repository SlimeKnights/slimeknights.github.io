Impaling

I'd be rather appalled if you impaled me with impaling.

Not to be confused with antiaquatic

Effects:

  • Increases damage of projectiles fired from crossbows
  • Multiple levels, each level increases damage further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Necrotic

Harvest the life of your foes in order to survive longer.

Effects:

  • Fully charged arrows steal life from your enemies to heal you
  • Multiple levels, increases amount of life steal per level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
