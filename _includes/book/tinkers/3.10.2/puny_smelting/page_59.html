Severing

Who knew you could blow heads clean off with some well placed explosives? Just a little off the top.

Effects:

  • Enemies drop their heads
  • Multiple levels, each level increases decapitation chance further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Sweeping Edge

Chim chiminey chim chiminey chim chim cher-ee, a sweep is as lucky as lucky can be.

Effects:

  • Targets of the sweep attack take a larger percentage of the tools damage
  • Multiple levels, each level increases percentage of damage further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
