Emerald

A villager's best friend, don't let the villagers see you applying this! These aren't your lucky charms.

Effects:

  • Increases tool durability depending on base durability
  • Increases conditional damage, mining speed, and knockback resistance
  • Single Level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Experienced

You feel a little smarter with some experience under your belt.

Effects:

  • Increases XP gain from blocks and mobs
  • Multiple levels, increases experience gained with each level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
