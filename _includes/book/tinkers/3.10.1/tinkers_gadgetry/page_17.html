Enderslime Islands

Enderslime islands can be found around the outer islands in the end as a breeding ground for enderslimes. I believe skyslimes and enderslimes were once the same before they came to our world, but living in their separate environments changed them.

Skyslime Geodes

Skyslime geodes are found in caves near the surface, and rarely even I have seen one on the surface leading me to believe they might have origins in space. These geodes seem to promote growth of certain varieties of moss nearby.
