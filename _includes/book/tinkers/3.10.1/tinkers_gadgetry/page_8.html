Invisible Ink

I once accidentally spilled an invisibility potion on my tool, and discovered the potion actually works to hide modifiers from view. The technique does not seem that useful unless a modifier does not go with the tools aesthetic, but maybe a more artistically minded Tinker will find a use for it.

Visible Ink

After some failure using sponges to make the modifier visible again, I remembered milk is effective at neutralizing potions. Sure enough, adding a bit of milk is all it takes to make the modifier once again visible.
