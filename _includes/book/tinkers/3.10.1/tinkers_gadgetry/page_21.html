Ichor Staff

Ichor is uncommon stuff, but allows making some of the strongest staffs. Grants higher velocity at the cost of drawspeed.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 3 Ability Slots

Enderslime Staff

Using the power of ender slime, you can make a staff for grabbing far away targets. Has stronger projectile power, but worse accuracy.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Defense Slots
  • 2 Ability Slots
  • Reach II
