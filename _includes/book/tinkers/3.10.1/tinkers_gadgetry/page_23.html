War Pick

The war pick is a combination of pickaxe and a crossbow. It can be found in mineshafts and obtained from villagers or illagers.

Properties:

  • Mines stone blocks and similar
  • Able to keep an arrow prepared to fire
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot

Battlesign

The battlesign is a combination of a melee weapon and a shield. It will typically be located in bastions, and can be obtained from piglins.

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • Medium Block Potential
  • 3 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
