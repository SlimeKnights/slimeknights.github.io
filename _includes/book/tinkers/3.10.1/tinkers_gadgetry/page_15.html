Skyslime Islands

Skyslime islands can be found around the overworld floating above the terrain, and contain grass which is essential for skyslime breeding. Legend tells that skyslimes were invaders from another realm, though they are mostly confined to areas near the islands.

Earthslime Islands

Earthslime islands are sometimes found in the overworld oceans, appearing less alien than their skyslime cousins. They are not the only way to find the common green variant of slime, though the grass causes them to appear much more commonly.
