Skyslime Staff

Skyslime staffs leave a lot of room for modification. Grants higher drawspeed at the cost of velocity.

Properties:

  • Low Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 2 Ability Slots

Earthslime Staff

Using the power of green slime, you can make the most defensive of magic staffs. Has higher block potential and accuracy, but worse projectile power.

Properties:

  • Mid Durability
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 3 Defense Slots
  • 2 Ability Slots
