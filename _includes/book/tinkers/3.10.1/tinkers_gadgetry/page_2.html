Slime Cake

I have found slimegrass works great as an alternative to wheat in cake, and liquid slime can be turned into a uniquely flavored frosting. While they are not the most tasty cakes, it seems I jump higher shortly after eating skyslime cake, and magma cake makes fire bother me less. I wonder what enderslime or earthslime does when consumed...
