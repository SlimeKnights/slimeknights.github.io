Haste

Adding redstone to a chestplate seems to make all tools faster, like your arms become more efficient.

Like really, crazy stuff, amiright?

Effects:

  • Increases mining speed
  • Multiple levels, each level increases speed a bit more
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Knockback

Stop wasting energy running and make your foes waste energy returning to you.

Effects:

  • Knocks back enemies on hit with any weapon
  • Multiple levels, increases force of knockback per level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
