Netherite

The strength of the nether flows intensely through your tool.

So much that the tool cannot be harmed by external damage such as lava.

Effects:

  • Boosts durability, attack damage, mining speed, toughness, and knockback resistance
  • Single Level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Overforced

Overforced... what a name, using too much force?

Effects:

  • Increases overslime capacity
  • Multiple levels, each level increases overslime capacity further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
