Composite Casts

Composite casting allows you to make new materials by pouring fluid over an item/block in a casting table/basin.

Notable materials are Slimewood and Nahuatl, which are made by pouring slime or molten obsidian, respectively, over wooden tool parts.

Automation

Casting basins and tables interact with hoppers, allowing items to easily be inserted and cooled items to be removed.

Comparators also make redstone turn funny colors when connected to a basin or table, and a redstone pulse can be used to swap the input and output.
