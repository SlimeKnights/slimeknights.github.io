Casting Tank

The casting tank is ideal for automatically filling or emptying simple fluid containers such as buckets, bowls, or bottles. Works with hoppers, faucets, and channels.

Interacting with the top allows placing items inside, and interacting wit the bottom lets you place fluids. Does not work well with tools, the tinker tank may serve you better.

Tier 2 General Materials

These materials can be casted into tool parts for melee weapons or harvest tools.
