Gauge

Ever wish you knew how much fluid was in a tank? Try a gauge! Gauges will tell you the amount of fluid inside a block when you interact with them.

Obsidian gauges will additionally show you the contained fluid in their window, though its not hard to look inside my tanks!

Fluid Cannon

Ever have too much molten metal and need something to do with it? Try the fluid cannon! On a redstone pulse, it shoots out contained fluid which might burn your enemies, destroy blocks, or automatically feed you!

Interacting with the bottom allows giving it an item stack to use with its fluid; with the right fluid type this can place blocks or use tools.
