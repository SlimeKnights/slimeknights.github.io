Sinistral

Double the bows double the trouble.

Effects:

  • Crossbows in main hand will fire with attack button, and will not reload when sneaking
  • Allows dual wielding crossbows, or using with a shield
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Trueshot

Zero in on your target.

Effects:

  • Increases accuracy of fired projectiles
  • Multiple levels, each level increases accuracy further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
