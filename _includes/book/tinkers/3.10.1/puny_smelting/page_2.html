The Melter

This here is your most basic melter able to perform a variety of simple tasks.

Features

• Early smeltery • Gives +3 nuggets or +1 gem per ore • Heater can be replaced with seared tank which can increase temperature • Internal capacity of 9 ingots • Melts 3 items at once • Very fuel efficient • Ideal for melting piglin drops such as gold swords or armor
