Hand Axe

The Hand Axe is a basic chopping tool. It is effective on wood and leaves. It works as a weapon or a tool, or both. The choice is yours.

Properties:

  • Basic mining tool
  • Effective on wood
  • Breaks leaves really fast
  • Strips woods logs with ease

Kama

The Kama is a precision reaping tool, fantastic at mowing down plants and shearing animals.

Properties:

  • Tills dirt
  • Interact with crops to harvest them
  • Shears blocks and animals
  • Effective on plants
  • AoE attack capabilities
