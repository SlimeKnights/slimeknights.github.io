Ranged Abilities

Abilities to become the best archer you can be These abilities allow your bow to do new and cool whacky things. Perhaps you will shoot crystals, or more than one arrow. Only time will tell.

Bulk Quiver

Putting a quiver on your back is overrated.

Effects:

  • Allows your bow to store arrows
  • Access by sneaking and attacking
  • Multiple levels, each level increases storage size
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
