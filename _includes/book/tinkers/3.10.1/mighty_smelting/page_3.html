Features

• Gives +3 nuggets or +1 gem per ore (by default) • Mass melting capabilities • Can hold multiple metals • Extendable capacity • Able to alloy • Fuel efficiency depends on size • Able to melt mobs to get their precious materials • Ideal for smelting lots of materials at once

Smeltery Controller

Using your melter, pour four ingots worth of copper on seared bricks to get your smeltery controller.
