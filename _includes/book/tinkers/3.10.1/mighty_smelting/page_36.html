Excavator

The Excavator is a broad digging tool. It harvests soil and snow in a wide range. Useful for when you need to dig a lot of graves.

Properties:

  • Advanced digging tool
  • 3x3 AoE Digging
  • Effective on dirt, sand and gravel
  • Can create path
  • Starts with Knockback II
