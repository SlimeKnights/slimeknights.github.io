Structure Requirements

Your smeltery has some requirements. Make it too big and it becomes so inefficient that you'd need a tower of fuel tanks bigger than the smeltery in order to run it.

Your smeltery must have a floor made of acceptable smeltery blocks excluding glass. Acceptable blocks will be showcased in a few pages! Your smeltery also must be rectangular, it can not be L shaped, circle shaped, or dodecahedron shaped.

The max size of your smeltery is 16x16x65 external, or 14x14x64 internal.
