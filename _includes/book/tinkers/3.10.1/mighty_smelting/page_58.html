Trick Quiver

Hawkeye's quiver on your bow.

Effects:

  • Gives the bow three toggleable arrow slots
  • Switch slots by attacking
  • Access the slots by sneaking and attacking
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Armor Abilities

Armor abilities are typically effective on a single type of armor. Each type of armor has a different special attribute:

• Helmets can be controlled using a keybinding for special effects. • Chestplates can receive interaction abilities that are used when hands are empty. Most are covered in the interaction section. • Leggings have inventory based abilities and passive boosts to the player. • Boots provide movement and jump based abilities. • Shields have special abilities on blocking, including stronger counterattack modifiers.
