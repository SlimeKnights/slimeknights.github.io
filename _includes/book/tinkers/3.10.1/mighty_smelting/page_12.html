Entity Melting

Entity melting is as simple as getting the entity inside your smeltery. The only requirement is that the smeltery has fuel. Almost any mob can be melted, some however will be much more useful than others. I have a challenge for the mightiest of Tinkers: if you can manage to melt the Ender Dragon, you will receive awesome power!

Notable Entities

• Silverfish - Seared Stone • Villagers and Illagers - Emerald • Skeletons - Milk • Perhaps there are even more to discover.
