Seared Tanks

Tanks are good for storing molten fluids or fuels. If you want to melt metals in your smeltery you'll need one! Tanks can be filled from buckets, potions, and can also be poured into from the smeltery if you want to store stuff for later or cast without a smeltery.

Tanks cannot be used for the floor of the smeltery.

Seared Blocks

These blocks can be used for any part of the smeltery, as floors or walls. They are also useful for decorating your base.
