Alloying

Alloying is a process that will allow you to combine your lesser materials into stronger, more powerful ones.

In order to alloy all you have to do is find two materials that can be alloyed together, put them in a fueled smeltery, and watch as they melt together to form a new stronger material.

Some materials may need hotter fuels in order to alloy.
