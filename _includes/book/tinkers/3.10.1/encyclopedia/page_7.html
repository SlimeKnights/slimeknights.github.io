Pickaxe

The Pickaxe is a precise mining tool, effective on stone, metal, and ores.

Expanders alternate between increasing depth and increasing height.

Properties:

  • +0.5 Attack Damage
  • 1.2 Attack Speed
  • Piercing I

Sword

The Sword is an all around weapon, granting medium speed and attack.

Expanders on a sword increase the area of the sweep attack, while Silky grants shearing.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 1.6 Attack Speed
  • 110% Durability Multiplier
  • Sweep Attack
