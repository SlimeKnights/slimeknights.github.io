Slurping

Allows drinking fluid contained in the helmet to perform effects using the helmet keybind, such as lighting yourself on fire, clearing status effects, or restoring hunger.

Higher levels consume more fluid to perform stronger effects

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank

Ambidextrous

Allows punching with the offhand. Offhand will receive all unarmed boosts applied to the main hand.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
