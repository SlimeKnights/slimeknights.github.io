Soulspeed

Grants +40.5% speed on soul sand, plus an additional 10.5% added for each additional level. Reduces the slowdown from walking on soulsand.

Boots will take damage overtime when this modifier is active.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Defense

Instead of material traits, armor will include defense slots to improve protection.

  • Fire Protection

  • Projectile Protection

  • Turtle Shell
