Molten Pewter

Molten pewter is probably lower risk than venom to boost your strength, but not by much.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • 7 seconds Strength

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Strength

Molten Gold

Molten gold first hurts you, then it heals you back more. Is it worth it? Up to you.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Magic Damage

  • 6 seconds Regeneration II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 6 seconds Regeneration II
