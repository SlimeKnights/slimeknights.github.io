Trick Quiver

Gives the bow three internal slots for holding trick arrows, each slot holding 32 arrows (or 64 arrows at level 2).

Can toggle selected arrow by attacking, between the three options or skipping the trick quiver. Sneaking and attacking opens the quiver to add arrows.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Armor Abilities

Armor abilities are typically effective on a single type of armor. Each type of armor has a different special attribute:

• Helmets can be controlled using a keybinding for special effects. • Chestplates can receive interaction abilities that are used when hands are empty. Most are covered in the interaction section. • Leggings have inventory based abilities and passive boosts to the player. • Boots provide movement and jump based abilities. • Shields are more effective at counterattacks, but some modifiers only work when blocking.
