Molten Steel

Molten steel lets you clear out enemies around you, or spread out bunched up enemies.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Repulsive II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Repulsive II

Molten Zinc

Molten zinc is probably not the best choice to boost your speed, perhaps the speed will throw off an opponent.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 10 seconds Speed

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Speed
