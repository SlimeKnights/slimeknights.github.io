Crystalshot

If there is no ammo in the inventory, bow produces its own crystal arrows.Tool takes an extra 4 damage per arrow produced.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Multishot

Allows shooting 2 additional arrows per level. Additional arrows location may vary based on the bow.

Must have additional arrows in the inventory to fire them, if too few the bow will shoot fewer arrows.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
