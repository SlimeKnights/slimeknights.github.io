Sledge Hammer

The Sledge Hammer is a broad mining tool, effective on stone in a 3x3 range.

Expanders alternate between increasing width and height.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 135% Attack Multiplier
  • 0.75 Attack Speed
  • 40% Mining Multiplier
  • 400% Durability Multiplier
  • Smite II

Vein Hammer

The Vein Hammer mines stone blocks and adjacent blocks of the same type, up to 2 blocks away.

Each expander increases the max distance of a block by 1.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 125% Attack Multiplier
  • 0.85 Attack Speed
  • 30% Mining Multiplier
  • 500% Durability Multiplier
  • Piercing II
