Milk

Milk is the cure to most ailments. In addition, when wearing skeleton it calcifies you, boosting skeleton traits.

Entity Effects:

  • Cures most status effects

  • +10 Seconds Calcified

Lava

Lava is a cheap, early source of boosting combat potential using fluids.

Entity Effects:

  • 1.0 Fire Damage

  • +4 seconds fire

Block Effects:

  • Places Fire
