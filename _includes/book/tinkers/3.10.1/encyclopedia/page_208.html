Molten Rose Gold

Molten rose gold lets you lose a small amount of health for a temporary edge.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage

  • 15 seconds Absorption II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 15 seconds Absorption II

Molten Electrum

Molten electrum is great for when you need to mine fast on a budget.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage

  • 8 seconds Haste

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Haste
