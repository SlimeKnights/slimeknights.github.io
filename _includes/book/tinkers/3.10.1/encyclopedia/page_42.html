Harvest Upgrades

Harvest modifiers are most useful on tools that break blocks, such as pickaxes or shovels.

Blasting

Grants more mining speed on blocks with lower blast resistance, up to +10 per level on the lowest blast resistance blocks. For example, grants +1 mining speed per level on blocks like stone, +3 per level on blocks like terracotta, and +9 per level on soft blocks such as ores.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
