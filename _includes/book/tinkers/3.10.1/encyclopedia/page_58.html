Feather Falling

Grants +15% resistance to fall damage per level, up to a maximum of +60% at 4 levels.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 4 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Lightspeed

Grants +9% movement speed per level in well lit areas. If the area is not as well lit, some boost will still be granted, but not as much.

Boots will take damage overtime when this modifier is active.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
