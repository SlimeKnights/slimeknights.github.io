Mattock

The Mattock is a farming tool, effective on logs, dirt, gravel, and sand. It is also faster on wood.

Expanders cause adjacent blocks of the same type to also be mined, with range based on the number of expanders.

Properties:

  • +1.5 Attack Damage
  • 110% Attack Modifier
  • 0.9 Attack Speed
  • 110% Mining Multiplier
  • 125% Durability Multiplier
  • Tills dirt

Pickadze

The Pickadze is a multipurpose stone and dirt mining tool, effective on a variety of blocks but unable to use harvest levels.

Expanders increase the height mined, allowing for effective staircases.

Properties:

  • +0.5 Attack Damage
  • 115% Attack Modifier
  • 1.3 Attack Speed
  • 75% Mining Multiplier
  • 130% Durability Multiplier
  • Makes paths
  • Harvest level limited to gold
