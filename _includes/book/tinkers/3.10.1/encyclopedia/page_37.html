Severing

Grants a 5% chance per level for a mob to drop its head or other body parts. Luck adds an extra 1% chance per luck level times severing level. Against rare mobs such as bosses, the chance is doubled.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Sweeping Edge

Increases the amount of damage dealt by the sword's sweep attack. Causes the tool to deal 25% of the main attack's damage at level 1, 50% of the damage at level 2, and 75% at level 3.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
