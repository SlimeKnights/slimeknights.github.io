Bursting

25% chance per level to drain a small amount of fluid (typically between 10 mb to 50 mb) to boost counterattacks with special effects. Blocking doubles the chance for the shield. Tool can be filled with fluid by interacting with a tank or drain while holding the tool.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank

Protection

Grants +5% resistance against all damage per piece with this modifier. Caps at 80% across the whole set, including other types of protection.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
