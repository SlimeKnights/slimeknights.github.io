Projectile Protection

Grants +8% resistance against projectile damage, such as arrows from skeletons. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 10 levels.

Grants +5% knockback resistance per level of the highest level piece.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level

Shulking

Grants +10% resistance while sneaking. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 8 levels.

Decreases attack damage while sneaking by 10% per level of the highest level piece.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
