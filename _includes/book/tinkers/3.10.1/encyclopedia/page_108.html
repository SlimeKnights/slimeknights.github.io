Boundless

Increases the cap from modifiers like protection, dragonborn, or shulking from 80% to 90% at 1 level, and 90% to 95% at 2 levels across. Stacks across multiple shields.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Reflecting

Projectiles are redirected in the direction you look when blocked within 2 seconds of deploying your shield. Each additional level grants an extra 2 seconds for reflecting.

Effects:

  • Requires 1 ability slot
