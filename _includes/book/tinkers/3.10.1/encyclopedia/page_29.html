Fireprimer

Increases the area of effect of flint and brick by 1. Equivalent to 1 level of expanded, but only requires an upgrade slot.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Magnetic

After mining blocks or attacking mobs (for tools) or passively (for armor), pulls items within 3 blocks towards the player, ignoring gravity.

Each level adds 1 extra block of range, and increases the speed.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level for armor per piece
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
