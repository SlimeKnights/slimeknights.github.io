General Upgrades

General modifiers are effective on multiple types of tools, including melee and harvest tools along with armor. Some modifiers are only to melee and harvest, so check the list of tools displayed.

Diamond

Grants +500 durability (+250 on armor), +0.5 attack damage, +2 mining speed, +0.5 projectile power, and +1 armor. Additionally boosts the tools harvest level to diamond.

Tools and armor will only receive applicable stat boosts, for instance held tools will not receive +1 armor.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
