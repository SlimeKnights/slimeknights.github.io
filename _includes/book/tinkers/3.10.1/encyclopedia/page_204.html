Molten Iron

Molten iron is used to draw nearby items towards you, though in a pinch it can be useful in combat.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Magnetic II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Magnetic II

Molten Pig Iron

Molten pig iron is the tastiest of molten metals. Not that its much of a competition.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • Restores 2 hunger
