Leaping

Grants +0.5 blocks jump height at first level, and +1 at second level. Increases the safe fall distance by 1 block per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • First level requires 1 upgrade slot
  • Second level requires 1 ability slot

Shield Strap

Adds +1 offhand slot per level, allowing swapping the offhand item by interacting with the leggings.

Effects:

  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
