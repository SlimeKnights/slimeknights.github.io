Swasher

The swasher is a combination of a melee weapon and a handheld fluid pistol. It is often found in buried treasure and shipwrecks, the hands of drowned, and sometimes caught when fishing.

Properties:

  • +0 Attack Damage
  • 1.5 Attack Speed
  • Spitting I
  • Spilling I
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot

War Pick

The war pick is a crossbow effective at mining stone, metal, and ores. It is found in mineshafts, pillager posts, zombie villagers, and a gift from weaponsmiths.

Expanders increase the circular radius of the pick.

Properties:

  • +0 Attack Damage
  • 1.2 Attack Speed
  • 1.0 Draw Speed
  • 1.00 Arrow Velocity
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
