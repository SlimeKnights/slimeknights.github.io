Molten Constantan

Molten constantan is useful against human opponents perhaps, but not so much against monsters.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • 10 seconds Hunger

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Hunger

Molten Lead

Molten lead gives your attacks a little extra boost, if you are willing to wait.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Poison

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Poison
