Slimeshell

The slimeshell harnesses the power of the shulker, increasing inventory and defense.

They are created by pouring enderslime on a shulker shell, and repaired with popped chorus.

Properties:

  • 630 Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Pockets
  • Shulking I

Slimeboots

Slime Boots are high mobility boots, with no protection or defense slots and high upgrade slots.

They can be created by pouring enderslime on a rabbits foot, and are repaired with rabbit hide.

Properties:

  • 546 Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Bouncy
  • Leaping I
