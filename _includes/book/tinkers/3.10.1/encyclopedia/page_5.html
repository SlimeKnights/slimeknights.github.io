Hand Axe

The Hand Axe is a precision chopping tool, effective on wood, plants, and leaves.

Expanders increase the circular radius of the axe.

Properties:

  • +6 Attack Damage
  • 0.9 Attack Speed
  • Strips wood

Kama

The Kama is a precision reaping tool, effective on plants, webs, and wool.

Expanders increase the circular radius of the tool.

Properties:

  • +1 Attack Damage
  • 50% Attack Modifier
  • 1.6 Attack Speed
  • Shears plants and animals
  • Small AOE Attack
  • Harvest plants
  • Tills dirt
