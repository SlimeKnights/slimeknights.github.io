Slurping

Allows drinking fluid contained in the tool to perform effects, such as lighting yourself on fire, clearing status effects, or restoring hunger.

Higher levels consume more fluid to perform stronger effects

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank

Spitting

Charging up then release to launch fluid inflicting effects on the target and consuming 1 durability. Higher levels fire multiple shots and consume more durability.

Power boosts the effect and fluid consumption by 25% per level. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
