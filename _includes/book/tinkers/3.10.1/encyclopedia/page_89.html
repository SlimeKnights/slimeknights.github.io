Warping

Charging up then release to teleport yourself in the targeted direction, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Melee Abilities

Melee abilities are most effective on tools built for melee combat, whether it's a sword or a repurposed harvest tool.

Melee modifiers applied to ranged weapons will only affect using the bow for melee unless otherwise stated.

Some melee abilities also work on chestplates. These will only affect attacks performed from an empty hand.
