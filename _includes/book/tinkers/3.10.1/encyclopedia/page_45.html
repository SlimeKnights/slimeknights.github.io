Fiery

Causes fired arrows to be ignited, burning targets they hit.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Does not work well with freezing
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Freezing

Freezes any entity hit by the arrow for 4 seconds (plus 4 seconds per level), causing damage over time and slowness.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Does not work well with fiery
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
