Freezing

Has a 15% chance per level to freezes the attacker for 2 to 8 seconds, causing damage over time and slowness. Multiple pieces will take the largest effect.

Shields are twice as effective but only work when blocking.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Does not work well with fiery
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Ricochet

Increases knockback received by 20% per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels per piece
  • Combines effect of all pieces
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
