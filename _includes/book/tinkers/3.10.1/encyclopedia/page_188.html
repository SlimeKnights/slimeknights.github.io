Blazing Blood

Blazing blood is an acceptable fluid for combat, but it really shines as a light source.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • +2 seconds fire

  • +5 seconds Glowing

Block Effects:

  • Places Glow

Powdered Snow

Powdered snow is a useful fluid for nether combat, dealing bonus damage to blazing mobs.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Cold Damage

  • +4 seconds freeze

Block Effects:

  • Places Snow
