Lightspeed

Grants +1 mining speed per level at light levels of 5, +3 mining speed per level at light levels of 10, and +9 mining speed per level at light levels of 15.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Ranged Upgrades

Ranged upgrades can be applied to longbows and crossbows to either boost the bow or the arrows fired.
