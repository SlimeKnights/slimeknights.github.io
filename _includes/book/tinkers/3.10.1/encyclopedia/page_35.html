Freezing

Freezes attacked mobs for 4 seconds (plus 4 seconds per level), causing damage over time and slowness.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Does not work well with fiery
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Knockback

Grants 1 knockback per level, which launches a mob roughly 2 blocks. Most tools start with 0.4 knockback.

Makes flinging, springing, and bonking 25% more effective per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
