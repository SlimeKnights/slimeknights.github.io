Melting Pan

The melting pan mines meltable blocks and also functions as a staff. It can be found from dungeons, igloos, husks, and a gift from toolsmiths after defeating a raid.

Expanders mine multiple blocks of the same type, with range based on the level.

Properties:

  • 6 Mining Speed
