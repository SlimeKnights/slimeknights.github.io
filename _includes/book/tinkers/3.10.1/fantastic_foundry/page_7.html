Byproducts

My Fantastic Foundry will give you byproducts from your ores. You may be asking 'But Nemea what are these byproducts?' Well just read a bit further and find out.

Byproducts are the hidden extras inside your ores that you would not normally be able to achieve with a simple furnace. A bit of extra material of some other kind that is hidden in the rock.

A couple of examples: copper ore gives gold as a byproduct, and cobalt ore gives iron as a byproduct.

Foundry Controller

Pouring one block's worth of obsidian from an alloyer on scorched bricks will grant you with this mystical Foundry Controller.
