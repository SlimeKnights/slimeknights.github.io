Structure Requirements

Your foundry has some requirements, bigger is not always better. Your foundry must have a floor made of acceptable foundry blocks excluding glass. Acceptable blocks will be showcased in a few pages! Your foundry also must be rectangular, it cannot be L shaped, circle shaped, or dodecahedron shaped. The max size of your foundry is 16x16x65 external. or 14x14x64 internal. The foundry must have corners and edges in order to function correctly.
