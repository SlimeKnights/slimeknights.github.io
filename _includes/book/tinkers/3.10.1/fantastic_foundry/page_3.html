Alloyer GUI

1: This area is where you see the materials in the side tanks, more tanks equals more spots for materials. 2: This is the area where your alloyed materials will show once they are finished working their magic. 3: This area is where your fuel shows. Hotter fuels means you can melt more things. In both 1 and 3, left clicking with a fluid item will attempt to fill the item from the tank, while right clicking will attempt to dump the item into the tank.
