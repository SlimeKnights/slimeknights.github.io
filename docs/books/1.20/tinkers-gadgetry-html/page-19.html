Slimy Armor

Mixing slime with various mob parts produces an interesting set of armor. Its not the most defensive, but it can really enhance your abilities.

  • Slimeskull
  • Slimelytra
  • Slimeshell
  • Slimeboots

Slimeskull

I have found enderslime can be used to mimic the effects of monsters with the proper focus such as a skull.

The skulls I have tested are described in the next section of the book.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
