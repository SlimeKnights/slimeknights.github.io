Ancient Tools

Ancient tools are tools rarely found in loot throughout the world. Their material construction appears to be random and cannot be altered, but they may be modified like any other tool. Rarely, they may come constructed from a unique material, which is only otherwise seen on armor or tool parts obtained from piglins.
