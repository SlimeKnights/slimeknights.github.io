Earthslime Geodes

Earthslime geodes are located deep underground, and can be some of the largest geodes I have seen. You can often identify them by the presence of clay.

Ichor Geodes

Ichor geodes are one of the best sources of the rare orange slime. They appear at high altitudes in the nether, and the crystals appear to glow as well.
