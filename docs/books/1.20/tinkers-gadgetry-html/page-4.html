Quartz Shuriken

Quartz is far lighter than flint, and far sharper. Shurikens carved from quartz I was finding were causing monsters much more damage, though less knockback.

Glowball

Combining glowstone and snowballs lets me create a throwable light source. It's effective for lighting up hard to reach areas, or finding danger hiding in a dark space without exposing yourself to harm.
