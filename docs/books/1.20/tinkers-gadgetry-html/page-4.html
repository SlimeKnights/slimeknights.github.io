Fancy Item Frames

Making use of rarer metals, I have managed to create variants of item frames with unique properties.

Fancy Item Frames

  • Clear: Turn invisible when an item is placed inside, and allow interacting with the block behind when filled and not sneaking.
  • Gold: Gradually rotates the item automatically. Crafting with a redstone torch reverses the direction.
  • Diamond: Gradually reduces the rotation until the item is upright.
  • Manyullyn: Always appears fully lit, even in darkness.
  • Netherite: Resistant to fire and explosions.

Diamond, manyullyn, and netherite also support twice as many rotations as other frames.
