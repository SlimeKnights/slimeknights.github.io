Staffs

Combining slime crystals and slime wood aids in creation of very flexible tools. Just need a bit of metal to hold the whole thing together.

  • Skyslime Staff
  • Earthslime Staff
  • Ichor Staff
  • Enderslime Staff

Skyslime Staff

Skyslime staffs leave a lot of room for modification. Grants higher drawspeed at the cost of velocity.

Properties:

  • Low Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 2 Ability Slots
