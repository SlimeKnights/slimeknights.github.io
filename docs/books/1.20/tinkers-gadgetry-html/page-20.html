Slimeskull

I have found enderslime can be used to mimic the effects of monsters with the proper focus such as a skull.

The skulls I have tested are described in the next section of the book.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot

Slimelytra

Tired of walking? Try flying with these futuristic wings!

To create these futuristic wings, you simply need to pour enderslime on an elytra. The slimelytra can later be repaired using phantom membranes.

Properties:

  • High Durability
  • No Armor or Defense Slots
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
