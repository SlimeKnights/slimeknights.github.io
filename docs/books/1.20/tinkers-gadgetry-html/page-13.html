Skyslime Geodes

Skyslime geodes are found in caves near the surface, and rarely even I have seen one on the surface leading me to believe they might have origins in space. These geodes seem to promote growth of certain varieties of moss nearby.

Earthslime Geodes

Earthslime geodes are located deep underground, and can be some of the largest geodes I have seen. You can often identify them by the presence of clay.
