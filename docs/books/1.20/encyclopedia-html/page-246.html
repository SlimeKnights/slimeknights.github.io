Crystalshot

If there is no ammo in the inventory, bow produces its own crystal arrows.Tool takes an extra 4 damage per arrow produced.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Grapple

Turns the fishing rod into a grappling hook, letting you pull yourself towards the block where your fishing hook lands. Allows the fishing hook to land in the side of blocks.

Grapple speed will be greater the further away you are from the hook when you reel it in.

Effects:

  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
