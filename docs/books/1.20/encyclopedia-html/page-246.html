Multishot

Allows shooting 2 additional arrows per level. Additional arrows location may vary based on the bow.

Must have additional arrows in the inventory to fire them, if too few the bow will shoot fewer arrows.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Returning

Causes the thrown weapon to return to you after a short time, phasing through any obstacle.

Projectile speed while returning is multiplied by the modifier level.

Effects:

  • Requires 1 ability slot for first level
  • Consecutive levels require 1 upgrade slot
  • Maximum of 4 levels
  • Requires throwing or a ballista bow
