Molten Enderium

Molten enderium is ideal as a weapon, keeping enemies from teleporting as you fight.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 4.0 Magic Damage
    • 10 seconds Enderference
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Enderference

    Molten Lumium

    Molten lumium is either used to keep your eye on the target or make a quick but obvious escape.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 4.0 Magic Damage
    • 5 seconds Glowing
    • 5 seconds Speed
    • 5 seconds Jump Boost
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Glowing and 5 seconds Speed and 5 seconds Jump Boost
