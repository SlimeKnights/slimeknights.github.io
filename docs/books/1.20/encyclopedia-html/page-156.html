Molten Silver

Molten silver removes toxins without removing helpful effects.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Magic Damage
  • Removes Wither

Molten Electrum

Molten electrum is great for when you need to mine fast on a budget.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage
  • 8 seconds Haste

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Haste
