Flint

Arrow Head

Tippable

Allows casting potion on the ammo to tip it. Tipped ammo will apply a potion effect in addition to its damage.

Wool

Arrow Head

Soft

Multiplies the projectile damage by 0. Note damage may still be increased by bow power, and it may still aggravate mobs.
