Bulk Quiver

Gives the bow internal slots for holding arrows. Arrows in the slots will be used if there are no arrows in the main inventory.

Quiver inventory can be accessed by sneaking and attacking. Inventory holds two stacks of 64 arrows per level.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Channeling

Adds a chance for lightning to strike thrown tools and fishing hooks based on the weather. In clear skies, has a 15% chance; 65% chance in the rain; and 100% chance in thunder storms.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Requires throwing, fishing, or a ballista bow
