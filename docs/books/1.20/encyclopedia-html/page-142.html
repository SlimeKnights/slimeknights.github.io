Molten Quartz

Molten quartz is a modest mining fluid, not too expensive and not too strong.

Entity Effects:

  • Mines up to hardness 5.0

Molten Emerald

Molten emerald is the choice for mining when you need silk touch.

Entity Effects:

  • Mines up to hardness 10.0 with Silk Touch
