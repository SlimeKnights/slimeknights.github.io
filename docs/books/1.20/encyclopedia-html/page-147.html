Skyslime

Skyslime is a portable piston, ideal for when you built your base just 1 block away from where it belongs.

  • Entity Effects:
    • +5 seconds Slowness
    • Pushes target
  • Entity Effects:
    • Pushes block

    Ichor

    Ichor is a reverse gravity fluid, helping you also reverse gravity. Surviving the trip back down is on you.

  • Entity Effects:
    • +7 seconds Slow Falling
    • +5 seconds Slowness
  • Entity Effects:
    • Places held block
