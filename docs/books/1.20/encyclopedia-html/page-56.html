Speedy

Multiplies speed by an extra 10% per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Step Up

Increases the height the player can step by 0.5 blocks per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
