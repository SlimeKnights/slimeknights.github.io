Molten Diamond

Molten diamond lets you maximize the drops of your ores, but is it worth the cost of the fluid?

Block Effects:

  • Mines up to hardness 10.0 with Fortune III

Molten Debris

Molten debris is the strongest fluid for mining, even capable of mining obsidian.

Block Effects:

  • Mines up to hardness 50.0
