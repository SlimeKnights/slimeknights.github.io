Molten Signalum

Molten signalum is great for inverted explosive combat with minimal environmental damage.

Entity Effects:

    Power 2 explosion

    Up to 2 Explosion Damage

    Inverted explosion knockback

Entity Effects:

    Explosion ignores blocks
