Embellishment

Allows changing the appearance of slimesuits, staffs, and shields.

Slime uses congealed slime blocks, while staffs and shields use wood embellishments.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Farsighted

Decreases the FOV by 5% per level while wearing this armor or holding this tool.

This modifier is ideal to cancel out the FOV change from the speedy modifier, but you may find other uses for it.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless
