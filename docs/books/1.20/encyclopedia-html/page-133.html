Water

Water is not the most useful fluid, but can protect you from fire in hot environments.

Entity Effects:

    2 Water Damage

    Extinguishes Fire

Entity Effects:

    Breaks blocks broken by water

Milk

Milk is the cure to most ailments. In addition, when wearing skeleton it calcifies you, boosting skeleton traits.

Entity Effects:

  • Cures most status effects
  • +10 Seconds Calcified
