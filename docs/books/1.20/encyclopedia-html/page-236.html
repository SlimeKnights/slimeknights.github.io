Spitting

Charging up then release to launch fluid inflicting effects on the target and consuming 1 durability. Higher levels fire multiple shots and consume more durability.

Power boosts the effect and fluid consumption by 25% per level. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Splashing

Drains a small amount of fluid (typically between 10 mb to 50 mb) on interaction to apply the special effects, such as fire, bonus damage, or potion effects to the block or entity target. Tool can be filled with fluid by interacting with a tank or drain while holding the tool.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank
