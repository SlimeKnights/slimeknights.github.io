Antiaquatic

Grants +2 damage per level against aquatic mobs.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Armor Pierce

Adds +0.5 melee damage per level. Melee attacks cancel out 1 point of armor per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
