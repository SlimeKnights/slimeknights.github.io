Molten Refined Glowstone

Molten refined glowstone is great for tracking targets without sacrificing much damage.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage

  • 10 seconds Glowing

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Glowing

Molten Refined Obsidian

Molten refined obsidian maximizes damage against armored targets.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Piercing Damage

  • 2 seconds Bleeding

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 2 seconds Bleeding
