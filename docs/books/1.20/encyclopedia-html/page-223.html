Farsighted

Decreases the FOV by 5% per level while wearing this armor or holding this tool.

This modifier is ideal to cancel out the FOV change from the speedy modifier, but you may find other uses for it.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Nearsighted

Increases the FOV by 5% per level while wearing this armor or holding this tool.

This modifier is ideal to cancel out the FOV change from the heavy tools, but you may find other uses for it.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless
