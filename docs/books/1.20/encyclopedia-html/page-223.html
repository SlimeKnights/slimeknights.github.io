Armor Trim

Combine armor trims with the correct material to add a colored variant of a unique texture to the armor.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Dyed

Changes the color of the leather or wood parts of travelers's gear, shields, and staffs.

Can mix multiple dyes, along with mixing dyes from the previous gear color.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless
