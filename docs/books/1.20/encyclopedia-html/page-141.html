Molten Glass

Molten glass can quickly solidify into an easily removable window.

Entity Effects:

  • 1.0 Piercing Damage

Block Effects:

  • Places Clear Glass

Molten Obsidian

Molten obsidian becomes a very difficult to remove blockage.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Piercing Damage

Block Effects:

  • Places Obsidian
