Molten Tin

They say carrots are good for your eyes, but molten tin is better.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Fire Damage
    • 8 seconds Night Vision
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Night Vision

    Molten Pewter

    Molten pewter is probably lower risk than venom to boost your strength, but not by much.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 3.0 Fire Damage
    • 7 seconds Strength
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Strength
