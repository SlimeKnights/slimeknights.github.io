Molten Silver

Molten silver removes toxins without removing helpful effects.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Magic Damage

  • Removes Wither

Molten Nickel

Molten nickel lets you harm your enemies and reduce the risk of retaliation, what's not to love?

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 7 seconds Weakness

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Weakness
