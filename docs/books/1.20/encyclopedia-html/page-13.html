Longbow

The Longbow is a broad ranged weapon that fires arrows. Arrows are launched immediately after releasing the longbow.

When using interaction modifiers, the longbow acts on left click.

Properties:

  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 1.0 Draw Speed
  • 1.00 Arrow Velocity
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • Sums stats of both limbs
  • 150% Durability Multiplier

Arrow

Arrows are a single-use projectile fired from longbows and crossbows. They cannot be used directly to attack.

Properties:

  • 2.0 Projectile Damage
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • 1 Head Trait, 1 Shaft Trait
