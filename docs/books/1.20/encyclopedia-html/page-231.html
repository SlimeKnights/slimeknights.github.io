Interaction Abilities

Interaction abilities change the way the tool interacts with blocks, entities, and even air.

Due to their nature, they will often severely conflict with other interaction abilities.

Many interaction abilities also work on ranged weapons, which are used by attacking instead of interacting.

Many interaction abilities also work on chestplates, requiring an empty hand to be used. They can be used from the main or offhand

Bonking

Charging up then release to knock the targeted mob away, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power or knockback, and halved by padded. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
