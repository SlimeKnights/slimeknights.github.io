Pathing

Flattens grass as you step, leaving a path behind. Expanded increases the area of effect.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Snowdrift

Leaves a trail of snow in cool biomes like a snow golem. Radius of trail can be boosted using expanded.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
