Slimeskull

The slimeskull is a helmet that copies abilities from monsters. It is created from pouring enderslime on a mob head, with the head type determining the effects.

The slimeskull section of the book has more information on variants.

Properties:

  • +362 Bonus Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • 1 Skull Trait
  • Mob Disguise
