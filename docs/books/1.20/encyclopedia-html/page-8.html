Broad Axe

The Broad Axe can mine a single column of a tree, or 6 blocks in a vertical column.

Expanders alternate between increasing width and depth.

Properties:

  • +5 Attack Damage
  • 165% Attack Multiplier
  • 0.6 Attack Speed
  • 30% Mining Multiplier
  • 425% Durability Multiplier
  • Strips wood

Cleaver

The Cleaver is a high damage, slow speed weapon.

Expanders increase the area of the sweep attack, and silky grants AOE shearing.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 150% Attack Multiplier
  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 350% Durability Multiplier
  • Severing II
