Molten Gold

Molten gold first hurts you, then it heals you back more. Is it worth it? Up to you.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Magic Damage
    • 6 seconds Regeneration II
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 6 seconds Regeneration II

    Molten Rose Gold

    Molten rose gold lets you lose a small amount of health for a temporary edge.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 3.0 Magic Damage
    • 15 seconds Absorption II
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 15 seconds Absorption II
