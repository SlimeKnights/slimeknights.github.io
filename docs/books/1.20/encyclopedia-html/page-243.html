Ranged Abilities

Ranged abilities can be applied to longbows and crossbows to either boost the bow or the arrows fired.

Ballista

Allows the bow to fire melee weapons from your other hand or quiver. Firing a melee weapon causes 3 damage.

Fired melee weapons ignore projectile power in favor of it's melee damage. Bow velocity boosts damage, and tool attack speed reduces movement speed.

Effects:

  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
