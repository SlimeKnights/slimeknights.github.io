Flinging

Charging up then release to launch yourself away from the targeted block, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power or knockback, and halved by padded. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Glowing

Causes the side of the targeted block to glow, giving off light. Placing a glow consumes 5 durability.On bows, will also place a glow at blocks targeted by the arrow, consuming the arrow in the process.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
