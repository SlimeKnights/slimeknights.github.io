Punch

Increases the knockback dealt by arrows and the distance fishing rods pull mobs each level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Quick Charge

Boosts the tool's drawspeed by 25% per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 4 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
