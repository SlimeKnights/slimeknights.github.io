Emerald

Grants +50% durability, +0.1 accuracy, and +1 toughness. Increases the tool's harvest level to iron, and boosts conditional attack damage and mining speed by 25%.

Tools and armor will only receive applicable stat boosts, for instance held tools will not receive knockback resistance.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Experienced

Boosts experience from killing mobs and breaking blocks by 50% per level.

On armor, boosts experience by 25% per level when using any tool.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
