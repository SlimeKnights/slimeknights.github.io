Tank

Increases the tank capacity of the tool by 1 bucket per level. Useful alongside abilities such as bucketing or melting.

Tools with tanks have the ability to be filled and drained by interacting with fluid tank blocks. On armor the piece must be held to fill or empty a tank.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Zoom

Allows quickly zooming in the field of view while sneaking and pressing the helmet keybind or by interacting with the held tool.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
