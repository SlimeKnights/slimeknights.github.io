Molten Copper

Molten copper is a good emergency air supply for long underwater journeys.

Entity Effects:

  • 1.0 Fire Damage
  • +3 Breath Bubbles

Molten Bronze

Molten bronze is the perfect dark brown beverage for when you need to stay away and stave off phantoms.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage
  • -5.00 m Time Since Last Rest
