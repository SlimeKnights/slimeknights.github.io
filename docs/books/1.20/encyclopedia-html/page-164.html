Molten Cobalt

Inhaling the lingering cloud is the safest way to use molten cobalt for haste, or just use it as a weapon.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 3.0 Fire Damage
    • 7 seconds Haste
    • 7 seconds Speed
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Haste and 7 seconds Speed

    Molten Hepatizon

    Is molten hepatizon best for defense, or combat? Either is a risk reward setup.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 4.0 Magic Damage
    • 8 seconds Wither
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Wither
