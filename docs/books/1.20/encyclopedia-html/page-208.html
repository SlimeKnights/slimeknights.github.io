Dragonborn

Grants +10% resistance while airborne. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 8 levels.

Boosts attack damage while in air by 5% per level of the highest level piece.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level

Fire Protection

Grants +10% resistance against fire damage, such as from lava or blazes. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 8 levels.

Reduces the duration of fire by 15% per level of the highest level piece.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
