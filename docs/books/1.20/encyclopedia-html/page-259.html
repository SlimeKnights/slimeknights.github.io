Tilling

Tills the earth beneath your feet. Area of effect expanded by the expanded modifier.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Boundless

Increases the cap from modifiers like protection, dragonborn, or shulking from 80% to 90% at 1 level, and 90% to 95% at 2 levels across. Stacks across multiple shields.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
