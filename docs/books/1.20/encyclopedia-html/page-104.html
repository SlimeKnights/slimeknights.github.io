Double Jump

Allows you to jump a second time in mid air. Higher levels add additional jumps, allowing triple jump or potentially even quadruple jump.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Flamewake

Ignites fires around where you step. Area of effect increased by expanded.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
