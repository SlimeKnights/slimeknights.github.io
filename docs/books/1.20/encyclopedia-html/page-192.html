Fiery

Causes fired arrows to be ignited, burning targets they hit for 5 seconds per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Does not work well with freezing
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Fins

Allows the tool when thrown or fired from ballista to move at full speed underwater instead of 60% speed.When used on bows, does not affect fired arrows. Bows only benefit if they themselves are the projectile.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
  • Requires throwing or a ballista bow
