Slimelytra

The slimelytra is the latest in flight technology.

Created by pouring enderslime on an elytra, these wings can also be repaired with phantom membranes.

Properties:

  • 672 Durability
  • 40% Unarmed Attack
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Wings

Slimeshell

The slimeshell harnesses the power of the shulker, increasing inventory and defense.

They are created by pouring enderslime on a shulker shell, and repaired with popped chorus.

Properties:

  • 630 Durability
  • 5 Upgrade Slots
  • 0 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • Pockets
  • Shulking I
