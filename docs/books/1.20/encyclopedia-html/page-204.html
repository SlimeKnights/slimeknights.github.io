Molten Cinderslime

Cinderslime flows the wrong way, and its explosion goes the wrong way.

Entity Effects:

  • Power 2 explosion

  • Up to 3 Explosion Damage

  • Inverted explosion knockback

Block Effects:

  • Explosion breaks blocks

Molten Queen's Slime

Sometimes you just want to blow up your enemies without blowing up your world.

Entity Effects:

  • Power 2 explosion

  • Up to 3 Explosion Damage

Block Effects:

  • Explosion ignores blocks

  • Explosion causes fires
