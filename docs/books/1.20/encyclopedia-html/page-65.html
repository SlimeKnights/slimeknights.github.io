Knockback Resistance

Reduces the amount of knockback received by 10%.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level per piece
  • Requires 1 defense slot

Revitalizing

Increases max health by 1 heart per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
