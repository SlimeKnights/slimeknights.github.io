Molten Electrum

Molten electrum is great for when you need to mine fast on a budget.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage

  • 8 seconds Haste

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Haste

Molten Aluminum

Molten aluminium is like milk, but aggressive. Ideal for combat when your opponents have too many effects.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Magic Damage

  • Cures most status effects
