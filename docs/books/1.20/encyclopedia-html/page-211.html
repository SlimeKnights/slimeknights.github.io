Turtle Shell

Grants +10% resistance when underwater. Caps at 80% across the whole set, or 8 levels.

Increases swim speed by 5% per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level

Special Defense Modifiers

Special defense boosts protection in ways other than raw defense.
