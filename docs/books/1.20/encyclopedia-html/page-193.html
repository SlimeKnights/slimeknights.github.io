Necrotic

Fully charged arrows heal a percentage of the damage dealt. Heals 25% at level 1, 45% chance at level 2, 60% at level 3, 70% at level 4, and 75% at level 5. Tool loses 1 extra durability per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Power

Increases projectile power by 0.5 per level, plus a bonus 0.5 for the first level.

Makes flinging, springing, bonking, and warping 25% more effective per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
