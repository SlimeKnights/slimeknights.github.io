Frost Walker

Causes water to freeze as you step on it. Area of effect increased by expanded.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Glowing

As you walk, places glows in dark nearby areas. Placing a glow consumes 5 durability. Range can be expanded using the expanded modifier.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
