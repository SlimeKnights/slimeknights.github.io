Molten Manyullyn

Molten manyullyn has the most damage potential between flat damage and damage over time.

Entity Effects:

  • 4.0 Fire Damage
  • 3 seconds Bleeding

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 3 seconds Bleeding

Molten Netherite

Molten netherite is the highest base damage, for when you cannot wait for your target to bleed out.

Entity Effects:

  • 5.0 Magic Damage
  • 5 seconds Blindness

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Blindness
