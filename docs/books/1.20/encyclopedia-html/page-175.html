Reinforced

Reduces the chance for the tool to take damage. Has a 75% chance of taking damage at level 1, 55% chance at level 2, 40% at level 3, 30% at level 4, and 25% at level 5.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Scope

Enlarges distant objects as the tool charges. Ends up less than a spyglass when fully drawn.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
