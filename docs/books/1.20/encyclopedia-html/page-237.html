Springing

Charging up then release to launch yourself in the targeted direction, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power or knockback, and halved by padded. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Stripping

Allows the tool to strip wood, similarly to hand or broad axes. Tools will use their normal mining area for stripping wood.

Triggers before the tool's native tilling or pathing if present.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
