Firestarter

Allows the tool to create fires on use, consuming 1 durability.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Flinging

Charging up then release to launch yourself away from the targeted block, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power or knockback, and halved by padded. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
