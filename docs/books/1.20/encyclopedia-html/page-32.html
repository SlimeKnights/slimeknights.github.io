Smelting

Cooks the items inside the tool when the tool is used to deal melee damage, mine blocks, fire arrows, or block damage. Cooks 1 item at a time.

Inventory can be opened by sneaking while interacting.

Effects:

  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Soulbound

Causes the tool to remain in your inventory upon death and respawn.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
