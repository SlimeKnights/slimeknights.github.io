Pickadze

The Pickadze is a multipurpose stone and dirt mining tool, effective on a variety of blocks but unable to use harvest levels.

Expanders increase the height mined, allowing for effective staircases.

Properties:

  • +0.5 Attack Damage
  • 115% Attack Modifier
  • 1.3 Attack Speed
  • 75% Mining Multiplier
  • 130% Durability Multiplier
  • Makes paths
  • Harvest level limited to gold

Pickaxe

The Pickaxe is a precise mining tool, effective on stone, metal, and ores.

Expanders alternate between increasing depth and increasing height.

Properties:

  • +0.5 Attack Damage
  • 1.2 Attack Speed
  • Piercing I
