Lava

Lava is a cheap, early source of boosting combat potential using fluids.

Entity Effects:

  • 1.0 Fire Damage

  • +4 seconds fire

Block Effects:

  • Places Fire

Blazing Blood

Blazing blood is an acceptable fluid for combat, but it really shines as a light source.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • +2 seconds fire

  • +5 seconds Glowing

Block Effects:

  • Places Glow
