Traveler's Pants

Traveler's Pants are a balanced set of leggings, with medium protection, and average upgrade and defense slots.

Properties:

  • 75% Durability Modifier
  • 75% Plating Stats
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 2 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • 2 Material Traits
  • Insulated

Traveler's Boots

Traveler's Boots are balanced boots, with low protection, and average upgrade and defense slots.

Properties:

  • 75% Durability Modifier
  • 75% Plating Stats
  • 2 Upgrade Slots
  • 2 Defense Slots
  • 1 Ability Slot
  • 2 Material Traits
  • Insulated
  • Powdered snow walking
