Reach

Increases the maximum distance from the player this tool can mine, place blocks, and attack mobs by 1 block per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Strength

Grants +10% attack damage to all weapons per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
