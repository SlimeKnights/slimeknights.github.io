Netherite

Grants +20% durability, +20% attack damage, +25% mining speed, +10% velocity, +5% knockback resistance, and +1 armor toughness. Increases the mining level to netherite, and makes the tool invulnerable when dropped.

Tools and armor will only receive applicable stat boosts.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot

Overforced

Grants +75 overslime per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
