Dyed

Changes the color of the leather or wood parts of travelers's gear, shields, and staffs.

Can mix multiple dyes, along with mixing dyes from the previous gear color.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Embellishment

Allows changing the appearance of slimesuits, staffs, and shields.

Slime uses congealed slime blocks, while staffs and shields use wood embellishments.

Effects:

  • Multi-use
  • Slotless
