Shuriken

Shurikens are quick, single-use ammo that can be thrown without requiring another tool.

This makes them easy to use, but limits their potential.

Properties:

  • 1.5 Velocity
  • 1.5 Projectile Damage
  • 200% Damage Multiplier
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • 2 Head Trait

Throwing Axe

Throwing axes are short range, high damage, single-use ammo that can be thrown without requiring another tool.

Ideal when you want to pack a bit more of a punch over a shuriken.

Properties:

  • 0.75 Velocity
  • 1.5 Projectile Damage
  • 300% Damage Multiplier
  • 0.5 Accuracy
  • 1 Head Trait, 1 Shaft Trait
