Enderslime Staff

Enderslime Staff is a high reach and power staff variant.

Properties:

  • 1520 Durability
  • 3 Projectile Power
  • 0.5 Accuracy
  • 3 Upgrade Slots
  • 1 Defense Slot
  • 2 Ability Slots
  • Reach II

Ancient Tools

Ancient tools cannot be crafted, instead they are found in the world in loot or bought from wandering traders. Their materials are random, and cannot be changed through part swapping.

Ancient tools typically have 2 materials from two different classes of tools (for instance, melee and ranged). In addition, they get stronger effects from the rebalanced modifier.
