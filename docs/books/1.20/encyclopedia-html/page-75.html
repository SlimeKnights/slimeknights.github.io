Ice

Arrow Head

Freezing

Freezes the target for 4 seconds (plus 4 seconds per level), causing slowness and damage over time.

Glowstone

Arrow Head

Spectral

Causes the target to glow for 10 seconds per level. If it hits a block, places a permanent light source at the cost of the projectile.
