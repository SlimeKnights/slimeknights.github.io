Kama

The Kama is a precision reaping tool, effective on plants, webs, and wool.

Expanders increase the circular radius of the tool.

Properties:

  • +1 Attack Damage
  • 50% Attack Modifier
  • 1.6 Attack Speed
  • Shears plants and animals
  • Small AOE Attack
  • Harvest plants
  • Tills dirt

Mattock

The Mattock is a farming tool, effective on logs, dirt, gravel, and sand. It is also faster on wood.

Expanders cause adjacent blocks of the same type to also be mined, with range based on the number of expanders.

Properties:

  • +1.5 Attack Damage
  • 110% Attack Modifier
  • 0.9 Attack Speed
  • 110% Mining Multiplier
  • 125% Durability Multiplier
  • Tills dirt
