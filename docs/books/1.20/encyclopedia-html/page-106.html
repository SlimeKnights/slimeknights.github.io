Long Fall

Neutralizes all fall damage.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires Feather Falling IV
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Pathing

Flattens grass as you step, leaving a path behind. Expanded increases the area of effect.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
