Respiration

Reduces air consumption to 50%, 33%, or 25% at level 1, 2, and 3 respectively.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Haste

Grants +10% mining speed per level for all held tools.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
