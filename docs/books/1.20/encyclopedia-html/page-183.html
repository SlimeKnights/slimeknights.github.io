Bane of Sssss

Grants +2 damage per level against arthropods and creepers.

In addition to arthropods and creepers, applies Slowness IV for 1 second, plus a a possible bonus half second per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Cooling

Grants +1.6 damage per level against mobs immune to fire.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
