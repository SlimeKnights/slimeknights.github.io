Wings

Allows you to glide by jumping while in mid air. Slowly damages the chestplate while gliding.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 2 ability slots

Crafting Table

Adds a 3x3 crafting table to the tool's inventory.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
