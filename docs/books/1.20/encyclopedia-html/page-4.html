Flint and Brick

Flint and Brick is a handheld tool starting with firestarter.

Expanded increases the radius of fire, which is further increased by fireprimer.

Properties:

  • +0 Attack Damage
  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 100 Durability
  • Fiery 1
  • Firestarting

Hand Axe

The Hand Axe is a precision chopping tool, effective on wood, plants, and leaves.

Expanders increase the circular radius of the axe.

Properties:

  • +6 Attack Damage
  • 0.9 Attack Speed
  • Strips wood
