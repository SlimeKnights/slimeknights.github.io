Parrying

Allows using the tool as a shield briefly after attacking. Most daggers can block up to 10 damage, and do not slow the player while using.

Effects:

  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Spilling

Drains a small amount of fluid (typically between 10 mb to 50 mb) to boost melee attacks with special effects, such as fire, bonus damage, or potion effects. Tool can be filled with fluid by interacting with a tank or drain while holding the tool.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank
