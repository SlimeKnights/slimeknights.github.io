Ranged Weapons

Ranged weapons are ideal for attacking enemies from a distance.

Many ranged weapons double as melee weapons, though they tend to be less effective. As a result, not every applicable modifier affects ranged attacks.

Crossbow

The Crossbow is a quick ranged weapon, capable of firing both arrows and fireworks. Arrows can be loaded into the bow then fired at a later time.

When using interaction modifiers, the crossbow acts on left click.

Properties:

  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 1.0 Draw Speed
  • 1.00 Arrow Velocity
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • 200% Durability Multiplier
