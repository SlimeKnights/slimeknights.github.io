Molten Nickel

Molten nickel lets you harm your enemies and reduce the risk of retaliation, what's not to love?

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage
  • 7 seconds Weakness

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 7 seconds Weakness

Molten Invar

Molten invar may be useful if you are trying to eat that powerful cake, but otherwise is an odd fluid.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage
  • 10 seconds Hunger

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Hunger
