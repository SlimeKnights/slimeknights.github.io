Molten Aluminum

Molten aluminium is like milk, but aggressive. Ideal for combat when your opponents have too many effects.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Magic Damage
    • Cures most status effects

    Molten Duralumin

    Disconnects you from the world.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 3.0 Magic Damage
    • 5 seconds Invisibility
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Invisibility
