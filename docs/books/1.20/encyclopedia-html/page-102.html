Pockets

Grants an extra 18 slots that can be accessed by sneaking and pressing the leggings interact key or while holding the pants.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 2 levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Soul Belt

Items in the hotbar remain in the inventory upon death and respawn.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
  • Requires Soulbound
