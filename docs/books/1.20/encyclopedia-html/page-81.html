Melting

Causes dropped items and attacked entities to be melted into the tools tank. Maximum fluid per hit produced at around 4 damage for most mobs. Also increases the tools tank capacity by 1000 mb. Melted fluid can be emptied by interacting with a tank or a drain while holding the tool or armor piece.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Unbreakable

Prevents the tool from taking damage, except from special damage sources such as tasty.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires Reinforced V and Netherite
  • Requires 1 ability slot
