Molten Zinc

Molten zinc is probably not the best choice to boost your speed, perhaps the speed will throw off an opponent.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Fire Damage
    • 10 seconds Speed
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Speed

    Molten Brass

    Molten brass helps you resist the heat, and the second sip is free if you time it right.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 3.0 Fire Damage
    • 8 seconds Fire Resistance
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 8 seconds Fire Resistance
