Swift Sneak

Reduces the sneaking movement speed penalty by 15% per level. At level 5, you may sprint while sneaking.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Depth Strider

Reduces the penalty of moving underwater by 33% per level. At level 3, there is no underwater movement penalty.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
