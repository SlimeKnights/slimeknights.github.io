Wetting

25% chance per level to drain a small amount of fluid (typically between 10 mb to 50 mb) when damaged to apply effects such as fire, healing, or clearing status effects. Blocking doubles the chance for the shield.

Tool can be filled with fluid interacting with a tank or drain while holding it.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank

Aqua Affinity

Allows mining at full speed underwater, instead of at 20% speed.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
