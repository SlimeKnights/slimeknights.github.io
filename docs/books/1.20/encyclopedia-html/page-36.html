Necrotic

Critical hits heal 5% of the damage dealt per level. Tool loses 1 extra durability per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Padded

Reduces knockback on the tool from all sources by 50% per level.

Makes flinging, springing, and bonking half as effective per level.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 3 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
