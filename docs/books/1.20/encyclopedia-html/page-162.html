Molten Platinum

Not sure the applications of molten platinum slowing your enemies' mining speed, but you can probably figure one out.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Fire Damage
    • 10 seconds Mining Fatigue
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Mining Fatigue

    Molten Tungsten

    Molten tungsten is one of the heaviest metals, hopefully it will keep your enemies still.

  • Entity Effects:
    • 2.0 Fire Damage
    • 10 seconds Slowness II
  • Entity Effects:
    • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Slowness II
