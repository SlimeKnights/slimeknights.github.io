Molten Osmium

Molten osmium keeps your enemies still for a future blow.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage
  • 10 seconds Slowness II

Entity Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Slowness II

Molten Uranium

Radioactive decay from molten uranium isn't safe to be around, so dump it on your enemies.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage
  • 10 seconds Poison

Entity Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Poison
