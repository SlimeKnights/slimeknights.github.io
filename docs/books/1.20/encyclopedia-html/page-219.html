Molten Lumium

Molten lumium is either used to keep your eye on the target or make a quick but obvious escape.

Entity Effects:

  • 4.0 Magic Damage

  • 5 seconds Glowing

  • 5 seconds Speed

  • 5 seconds Jump Boost

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Glowing and 5 seconds Speed and 5 seconds Jump Boost

Molten Nicrosil

Nicrosil has an innate pull on magic, seems like a waste to use it like this.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Magic Damage

  • 5 seconds Experienced

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Experienced
