Arrow

Arrows are your basic projectile for most bows. While you can use the basic arrows dropped from skeletons, these are better.

Properties:

  • Requires a bow or crossbow
  • Improvement over skeleton arrows
  • Has different effects based on materials

Shuriken

Shurikens are a quick, disposable ammo meant for keeping monsters at bay.

They can't receive many modifiers, but can still provide decent damage.

Properties:

  • Quick, disposable ranged option
  • Does not require another tool to fire
