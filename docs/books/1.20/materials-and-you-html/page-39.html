Crossbow

The crossbow is useful for those wanting to stay quick and ready for a fight

You should never cross a bow

Properties:

  • Quick and nimble type of bow
  • Able to keep an arrow prepared to fire
  • Likes to party with fireworks

Fishing Rod

The fishing rod is useful for fighting close-distance targets without wasting ammo. It also catches fish as a bonus if used on water.

Properties:

  • Short distance ranged weapon
  • Damages monsters when reeled back
  • Requires no ammo
  • Can catch fish
