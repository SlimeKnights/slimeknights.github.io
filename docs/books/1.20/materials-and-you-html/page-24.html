Sword

The sword is a versatile weapon. It has average damage and allows you to knock back multiple enemies at once with a sweep attack, all while keeping your other hand free.

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • Sweep attack

Crossbow

The crossbow is useful for those wanting to stay quick and ready for a fight

You should never cross a bow

Properties:

  • Quick and nimble type of bow
  • Able to keep an arrow prepared to fire
  • Likes to party with fireworks
