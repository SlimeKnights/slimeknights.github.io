Dagger

The dagger is a versatile weapon for the quick warrior. It has low damage with higher speed allowing for quick fast attacks. The dagger is also able to be used in your off-hand for double the daggers.

Properties:

  • Low Damage
  • High Speed
  • Able to use in off-hand
  • Stacks to 2 when crafting and modifying

Flint and Brick

Flint and Brick is an affordable tool effective for starting fires.

Unlike most other tools, it is crafted in a crafting station or crafting table.

Properties:

  • Creates fires
  • Starts with fiery 1
  • Repaired using seared or scorched bricks
