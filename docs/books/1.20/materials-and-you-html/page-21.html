Flint and Brick

Flint and Brick is an affordable tool effective for starting fires.
Unlike most other tools, it is crafted in a crafting station or crafting table.

Properties:

  • Creates fires
  • Starts with fiery 1
  • Repaired using seared or scorched bricks

Hand Axe

The Hand Axe is a basic chopping tool. It is effective on wood and leaves. It works as a weapon or a tool, or both. The choice is yours.

Properties:

  • Basic mining tool
  • Effective on wood
  • Breaks leaves really fast
  • Strips woods logs with ease
