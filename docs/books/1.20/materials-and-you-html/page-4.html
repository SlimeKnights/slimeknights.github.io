Storage Chests

So, you want to store your extra parts in a special chest, eh? If not, too bad you have the option anyway.

Part chests allow you to store an abundance of extra parts you may have. Each slot can hold a stack of 8 parts max. If you need more than that, you're doing something wrong or have very convoluted recipes.

Oh, that chest is too restrictive? Try the tinkers' chest! It can store any item in a total of 64 slots, though each slot holds just 16 items.
