Fishing Rod

The fishing rod is useful for fighting close-distance targets without wasting ammo. It also catches fish as a bonus if used on water.

Properties:

  • Short distance ranged weapon
  • Damages monsters when reeled back
  • Requires no ammo
  • Can catch fish

Arrow

Arrows are your basic projectile for most bows. While you can use the basic arrows dropped from skeletons, these are better.

Properties:

  • Requires a bow or crossbow
  • Improvement over skeleton arrows
  • Has different effects based on materials
