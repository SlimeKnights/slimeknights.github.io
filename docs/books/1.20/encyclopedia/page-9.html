Cleaver

The Cleaver is a high damage, slow speed weapon.

Expanders increase the area of the sweep attack, and silky grants AOE shearing.

Properties:

  • +3 Attack Damage
  • 150% Attack Multiplier
  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 350% Durability Multiplier
  • Severing II

Excavator

The Excavator is a broad digging tool, effective on dirt, gravel, and sand in a 3x3 range.

Expanders alternate between increasing width and height.

Properties:

  • +1.5 Attack Damage
  • 120% Attack Multiplier
  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 30% Mining Multiplier
  • 375% Durability Multiplier
  • Knockback II
  • Makes path
