Molten Amethyst Bronze

Sometimes you really want to fight some phantoms, drinking a lot of molten amethyst bronze can help that happen.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • +1.67 m Time Since Last Rest

Molten Iron

Molten iron is used to draw nearby items towards you, though in a pinch it can be useful in combat.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Magnetic II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Magnetic II
