Splashing

Drains a small amount of fluid (typically between 10 mb to 50 mb) on interaction to apply the special effects, such as fire, bonus damage, or potion effects to the block or entity target. Tool can be filled with fluid by interacting with a tank or drain while holding the tool.

Effects:

  • No max level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
  • Fill by using tool on a fluid tank

Springing

Charging up then release to launch yourself in the targeted direction, consuming 1 durability.

Effect is boosted by 25% per level of power or knockback, and halved by padded. Charge time is reduced by quick charge.

Effects:

  • Maximum of 1 level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
