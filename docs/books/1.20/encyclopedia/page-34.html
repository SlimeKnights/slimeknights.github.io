Melee Upgrades

Melee upgrades are most effective on tools built for melee combat, whether it is a sword, a repurposed harvest tool, or a bow for stabbing.

When applied to ranged weapons such as bows or crossbows, these modifiers will boost the effectiveness of the weapon for melee attacks only unless otherwise stated.

Many of these upgrades can also be applied to chestplates with the unarmed modifier. They will only boost attacks with an empty hand.

Fiery

Causes attacked mobs to burn, for 5 seconds per level.

Effects:

  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Maximum of 5 levels
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
