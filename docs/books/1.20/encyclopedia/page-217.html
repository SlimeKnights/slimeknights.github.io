Molten Hepatizon

Is molten hepatizon best for defense, or combat? Either is a risk reward setup.

Entity Effects:

  • 4.0 Fire Damage

  • 15 seconds Resistance

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 15 seconds Resistance

Molten Manyullyn

Molten manyullyn has the most damage potential between flat damage and damage over time.

Entity Effects:

  • 4.0 Fire Damage

  • 2 seconds Bleeding

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 2 seconds Bleeding
