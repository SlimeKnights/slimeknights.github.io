Molten Tungsten

Molten tungsten is one of the heaviest metals, hopefully it will keep your enemies still.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 10 seconds Slowness II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Slowness II

Molten Osmium

Molten osmium keeps your enemies still for a future blow.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 10 seconds Slowness II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 10 seconds Slowness II
