Molten Pig Iron

Molten pig iron is the tastiest of molten metals. Not that its much of a competition.

Entity Effects:

  • 3.0 Fire Damage

  • Restores 2 hunger

Molten Steel

Molten steel lets you clear out enemies around you, or spread out bunched up enemies.

Entity Effects:

  • 2.0 Fire Damage

  • 5 seconds Repulsive II

Block Effects:

  • Lingering cloud with 5 seconds Repulsive II
