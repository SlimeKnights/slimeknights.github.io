Longbow

The Longbow is a broad ranged weapon that fires arrows. Arrows are launched immediately after releasing the longbow.

When using interaction modifiers, the longbow acts on left click.

Properties:

  • 1.0 Attack Speed
  • 1.0 Draw Speed
  • 1.00 Arrow Velocity
  • 0.75 Accuracy
  • Sums stats of both limbs
  • 150% Durability Multiplier

Scythe

The Scythe is a broad reaping tool, effective on plants or monsters in a 3x3x3 range.

Expanders alternate between increasing width/height and depth, while silky grants AOE shearing.

Properties:

  • +1 Attack Damage
  • 0.7 Attack Speed
  • 45% Mining Multiplier
  • 250% Durability Multiplier
  • AOE Attack
  • Tills dirt
  • Harvest plants
