Javelin

The Javelin is a hybrid melee/ranged weapon, providing strong close ranged attacks and distance.

Just be careful when throwing as you leave yourself without a weapon!

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • Medium Speed
  • Throwable

Longbow

The longbow is useful for those ready to stand their ground and fight with power

Longer than a bow, but shorter than a long long bow

Properties:

  • A slower but more powerful bow
  • Designed for higher damage
