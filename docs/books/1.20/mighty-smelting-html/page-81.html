Turtle Shell

Does not protect you from green, red, or blue shells.

Effects:

  • Decreases damage underwater and increases swim speed
  • Multiple levels, each level further boosts the effect
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level

Special Defense Modifiers

Special defense boosts protection in ways other than raw defense.
