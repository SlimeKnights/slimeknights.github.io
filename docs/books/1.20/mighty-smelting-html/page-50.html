Splashing

Lok a pool party, but without the pool.

Right clicking a tank with tool fills it.

Effects:

  • Drains fluid on interaction
  • Various effects based on fluid, including fire and poison
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Springing

The first part of daylight savings time.

Effects:

  • Charge to launch in the direction you are looking
  • Can boost launch speed with power or knockback
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
