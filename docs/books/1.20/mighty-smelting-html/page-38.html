Cleaver

The Cleaver is a high damage, slow swinging weapon.

Off with their heads!

Properties:

  • Strong Weapon
  • Slow Swinging
  • Starts with Severing II

Excavator

The Excavator is a broad digging tool. It harvests soil and snow in a wide range.

Useful for when you need to dig a lot of graves.

Properties:

  • Advanced digging tool
  • 3x3 AoE Digging
  • Effective on dirt, sand and gravel
  • Can create path
  • Starts with Knockback II
