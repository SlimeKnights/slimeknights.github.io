Vein Hammer

The vein hammer is a broad mining tool. It harvests blocks of the same type in veins.
It is optimal for getting all ores in a vein in one hit.
Disclaimer: Thruul Industries is not responsible for mass vein mining of property.

Properties:

  • Advanced mining tool
  • Vein mining
  • Effective on stone and ores
  • Bonus piercing damage

Javelin

The Javelin is a hybrid melee/ranged weapon, providing strong close ranged attacks and distance.

Just be careful when throwing as you leave yourself without a weapon!

Properties:

  • Medium Damage
  • Medium Speed
  • Throwable
