Shulking

Home is where the shell is.

Effects:

  • Reduces damage given and received while sneaking
  • Multiple levels, each level further increases effect
  • Can apply levels incrementally
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level

Turtle Shell

Does not protect you from green, red, or blue shells.

Effects:

  • Decreases damage underwater and increases swim speed
  • Multiple levels, each level further boosts the effect
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
