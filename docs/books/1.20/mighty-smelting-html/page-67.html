Double Jump

Bunny hopping increases your skills and makes you way better at the game. Don't forget to throw in some proning for maximum effect.

Effects:

  • Allows an extra jump
  • Multiple levels, each level gives an extra jump
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Flamewake

  • Requires 1 ability slot
