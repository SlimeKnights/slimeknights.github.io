Interaction Abilities

These abilities all affect your interactions.

Due to their nature, some interaction abilities will conflict.

Bonking

Best used on cliffs during epic sunset.

Effects:

  • Charge to launch the targeted mob away
  • Can boost launch speed with power or knockback
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
