Protection Defense Modifiers

Protection defense modifiers increase resistance to a particular type of damage. All damage sources should fit into one of the five types, and types can be freely mixed on armor and shields for passive protection.

Blast Protection

Creeper, Aw man.

Effects:

  • Reduces explosive damage and knockback
  • Multiple levels, each level further increases resistance
  • Requires 1 defense slot per level
