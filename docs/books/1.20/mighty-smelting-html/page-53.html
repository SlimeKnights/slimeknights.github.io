Spitting

Never get into a spitting contest with a llama

Effects:

  • Charge to fire fluid in the direction looked
  • Higher levels launch more projectiles
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Splashing

Lok a pool party, but without the pool.

Right clicking a tank with tool fills it.

Effects:

  • Drains fluid on interaction
  • Various effects based on fluid, including fire and poison
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
