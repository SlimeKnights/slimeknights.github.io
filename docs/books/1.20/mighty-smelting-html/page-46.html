Gilded

It's a 2 for 1 special!

Effects:

  • Trades 1 ability slot for 2 upgrade slots
  • Multiple levels
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Lucky

In my experience, there's no such thing as luck.

Effects:

  • Increases mob drops and block drops
  • On tools, max of 3 levels, different recipe for each level
  • On leggings, max of 1 level, boosts the drops for the held tool
  • Only first level requires 1 ability slot
