Throwing

How effective is throwing your sword? Way more effective than throwing your bow!

Effects:

  • Allows throwing tools
  • Hit monsters for damage!
  • Hitting effective blocks may break them!
  • Must retrieve manually
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Tilling

Don't waste diamonds on a hoe.

Effects:

  • Allows you to till the ground with your tool
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
