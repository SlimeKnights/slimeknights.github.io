Reach

Reach for the stars! Or the top shelf... Or the medium height shelf if you are short...

Effects:

  • Increases reach distance for blocks, mining, and attacking
  • Multiple levels, each level allows you to reach further
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level

Strength

With great power, comes greater damage.

Effects:

  • Increases attack damage of weapons
  • Multiple levels, each level increases damage further
  • Requires 1 ability slot per level
