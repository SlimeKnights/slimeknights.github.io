Pathing

Tired of forgetting the way back home? Why not leave a constant trail? Trample the earth as you walk and never forget where you have been!

Effects:

  • Creates paths as you walk
  • Area boosted by expanded
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot

Snowdrift

Look at the snow golem. Isn't he so cool, leaving trails of snow? Don't you wish you could leave trails of snow too?

Effects:

  • Creates snow as you walk
  • Does not work in warm biomes
  • Area boosted by expanded
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 ability slot
