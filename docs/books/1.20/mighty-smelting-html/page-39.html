Scythe

The Scythe is an adventurers broadest friend. Prefer venturing through the thick jungles, delving into the deep spider infested caves, or just harvesting your crops? The scythe will always have your back for hacking through foliage.

Properties:

  • Advanced harvesting tool
  • 3x3 AoE harvesting and tilling
  • Effective on all types of foliage
  • AoE attack capabilities

Sledge Hammer

The Sledge Hammer is a broad mining tool. It harvests blocks in a wide range and is effective against undead.
It is optimal for mining out large areas and long mining trips.

Properties:

  • Advanced mining tool
  • 3x3 AoE Mining
  • Effective on stone and ores
  • Starts with Smite II
