Seared Ladder

The seared ladder can be used to easily climb up or out of your smeltery, useful for if you plan to build it bigger later!

Seared Glass

Looking at your mighty marvels of molten materials without invalidating your smeltery can be done with this glass. Do note it doesn't store fluid unlike tanks.

Glass cannot be used for the floor of the smeltery.
