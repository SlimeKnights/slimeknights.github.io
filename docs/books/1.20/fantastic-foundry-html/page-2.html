Alloy Ahead!

Features

Since my foundry can't alloy I have created a solution for you that is simple to use.

  • Requires a fuel tank or a heater, better fuel allows making better alloys
  • Takes input from tanks around the alloyer, adding more tanks gives you more metals to alloy with at once
