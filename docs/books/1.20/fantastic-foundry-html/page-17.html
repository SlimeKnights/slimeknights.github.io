Melting

Foundry not portable enough? Try turning your tool into a handheld foundry! Perhaps Thruul is smart enough to make something worthwhile after all.

Effects:

  • Melts attacked mobs into the tool's tank
  • Dropped items also are melted if possible
  • Tool can be emptied into a tank or a smeltery
  • Requires 1 ability slot, single level

Bartering
