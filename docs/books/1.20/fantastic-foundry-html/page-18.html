Ichor

You can get blazing blood from melting ichor, though this is less ideal as you need a hotter fuel to melt it in the first place. It is ultimately a net gain though so it could be good to increase your supply after you get blazing blood from another source.

Tier 4 General Materials

These materials require blazing blood to create, and can be used to create tool parts for melee weapons or harvest tools.
