Tinted Scorched Glass

Want to keep your foundry dark to maximize blaze spawns? Consider scorched tinted glass as a window instead of standard tinted glass, totally transparent but also does not let light through.

Glass cannot be used for the floor of the foundry.

Scorched Soul Glass

Looking not good enough, want to touch the molten metals? Swim in them? Soul glass creates a barrier you can pass but the fluids cannot.

Glass cannot be used for the floor of the foundry.
