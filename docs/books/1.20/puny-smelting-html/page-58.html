Antiaquatic

The enemies of the sea tremble in fear from those wielding weapons with this trait. Disclaimer: You are a horrible person if you use this on peaceful sea life.

Not to be confused with impaling.

Effects:

  • Increases damage to sea creatures
  • Multiple levels, each level increases damage further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Armor Pierce

Tool gains the power of the cactus, attacking through armor.

Not to be confused with impaling

Effects:

  • Cancels out a small amount of armor
  • Also slightly boosts the tool's damage
  • Multiple levels, each level increases effect
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
