Barebow

Whoever said bear archery was a good idea?

Effects:

  • Bow will not use ammo from the inventory
  • Useful for preferring crystalshot or quivers
  • Single use
  • Slotless

Blindshot

I'm going to put some dirt in your eye.

Effects:

  • Decreases accuracy of fired projectiles
  • Multiple levels, each level decreases accuracy further
  • Slotless
