Punch

Here comes the punchline.

Effects:

  • Increases knockback on fired projectiles
  • Multiple levels, each level increases knockback further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Quick Charge

Become rather high strung.

Effects:

  • Increases drawspeed of crossbows or staffs
  • Multiple levels, each level increases drawspeed further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
