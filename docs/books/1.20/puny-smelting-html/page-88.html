Worldbound

Fire is no longer your enemy, your tool feels comfortable with the world around it.

Effects:

  • Crude version of netherite
  • Grants immunity to external damage but no strength increase
  • Single Use
  • Requires no slot

Damage Tools

Ever thought to yourself "I wish I could break my tool faster"? Just pour some magma on it to deplete a small amount of durability, or use venom for a larger amount. Still not enough? Pour lava or blazing blood on it to really destroy the tool.
