Item Frame

Tired of holding your clock and compass? Why not wear it instead?

Effects:

  • Adds slots to the helmet, interact to access
  • Helmet will render items when worn
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Minimap

Display the map up on your head, making it a head's up display.

Effects:

  • Adds slots to the helmet, interact to access
  • Helmet will explore map when worn
  • Swap map with control + helmet interact
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
