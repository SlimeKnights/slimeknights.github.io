Bounce

The ultimate trick shot. Shoot around corners or over obstacles. Just be careful your arrow doesn't bounce back at you!

Effects:

  • Causes arrows to bounce off blocks
  • Multiple levels, each level adds more bounces
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
