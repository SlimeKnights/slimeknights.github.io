Damage Tools

Ever thought to yourself "I wish I could break my tool faster"? Just pour some magma on it to deplete a small amount of durability, or use venom for a larger amount. Still not enough? Pour lava or blazing blood on it to really destroy the tool.

Extra Modifiers

Everyone loves extra modifier slots! These special modifiers will increase your modifying potential.

These modifiers can be applied to give you some extra slots for your tool! Now you can get everything you wanted without having to make a choice.
