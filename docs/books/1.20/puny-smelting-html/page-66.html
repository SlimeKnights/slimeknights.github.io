Lightspeed

The light flows through your tool and helps the tool mine faster.

Effects:

  • Mines well lit blocks faster
  • Multiple levels, increases speed further with each level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Ranged Upgrades

Ranged upgrades are good for bows, whether it's quicker firing, more damage, or freezing your enemies.
