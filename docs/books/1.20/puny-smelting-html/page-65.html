Haste

Adding redstone to a tool seems to increase its speed. The mining speed boost is more potent on slower tools.

Redstone. Crazy stuff, amiright?

Effects:

  • Increases mining speed
  • Multiple levels, each level increases speed a bit more
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Hydraulic

Using the magic of pumps and fluids you can move things with ease. I mean you can just not in this case.

Effects:

  • Mines faster underwater or in the rain
  • Multiple levels, increases speed further with each level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
