Workbench

Sometimes, you don't want to stop ruffling through your pockets long enough to craft.

Effects:

  • Adds an 2x2 crafting table to the tool's inventory
  • Single level
  • Slotless

Worldbound

Fire is no longer your enemy, your tool feels comfortable with the world around it.

Effects:

  • Crude version of netherite
  • Grants immunity to external damage but no strength increase
  • Single Use
  • Requires no slot
