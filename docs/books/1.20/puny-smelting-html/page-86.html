Offhanded

The most nonchalant modifier.

Effects:

  • Disables tool's interaction when offhand is occupied
  • Second level fully disables interactions
  • Maximum of 2 levels

Overslime

This upgrade is a bit over the top, wouldn't you say?

Effects:

  • Adds an overslime bar, used before durability
  • Recharged with slimeballs, or bottles in the crafting table
  • Capacity is boosted by many tool materials
  • Applies debuff to tools without slimy materials
