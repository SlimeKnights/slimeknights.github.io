Leaping

Leap for the stars, and if you cannot reach them, climb a mountain then try and reach them.

Effects:

  • Increases jump height and safe fall height
  • Multiple levels, each level increases jump and fall damage height further
  • First level requires 1 upgrade slot
  • Second level requires 1 ability slot

Shield Strap

He protec, he attac, but most importantly he bring the torches bac.

Effects:

  • Grants an extra offhand slot
  • Leggings interact allows swapping offhand
  • Multiple levels, each level gives +1 slot
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
