Knockback

Stop wasting energy running and make your foes waste energy returning to you.

Effects:

  • Knocks back enemies on hit with any weapon
  • Multiple levels, increases force of knockback per level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Sleeves

Hidden shurikens, the classic ninja trick.

Effects:

  • Throw items from empty hand by interacting
  • Sneak to toggle selected item
  • Right click chestplate in inventory to access
  • Multiple levels, each level adds more slots
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
