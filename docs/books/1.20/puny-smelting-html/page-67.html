Fiery

The fires of the nether work to punish your foes.

Effects:

  • Burns targets hit by the arrow
  • Multiple levels, higher levels increase burn duration
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Freezing

Elsa may be jealous.

Effects:

  • Fired projectiles slows enemies and deal extra damage
  • Multiple levels, each level slows enemies for longer
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
