Fiery

The fires of the nether work to punish your foes.

Effects:

  • Burns targets hit by the arrow
  • Multiple levels, higher levels increase burn duration
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Fins

Turns your sword into a fish. A swordfish?

Effects:

  • Allows the thrown tool to move freely underwater
  • Single level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot
  • Requires throwing or a ballista bow
