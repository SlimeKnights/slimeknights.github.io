Visible Ink

Oh, changed your mind and want to see the ugly modifier again? Luckily for indecisive you, a bucket of milk is enough to make the modifier visible again.

Sort Modifiers

While normally compass magnetizes tools, you can instead use the magnet to reorder modifiers without removing them. Unlike invisible ink, this isn't just art; the order of modifiers on the tool allows you to change the order overlapping modifiers run. Placing the compass in the upper slot makes the modifier lower priority, while the lower slot makes it higher.
