Embellishment

The original cosmetic to change the appearance of tools and armor!

Effects:

  • Changes the appearance of armor, shields, and staffs
  • Use wood to embellish staffs and shields
  • Use congealed slime to embellish slime
  • Multi-use
  • Slotless

Farsighted

Manyullyn is a great material, but there are many good materials that are easier to access, take some time and try them out before rushing to the end.

Effects:

  • Decreases FOV on the held tool or worn armor
  • Multiple levels
  • Slotless
