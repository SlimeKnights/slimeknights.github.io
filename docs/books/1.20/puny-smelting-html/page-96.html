Invisible Ink

Are the modifiers on your tool ugly? Just pour a bottle of skyslime on it! For some sciency reason, the slime interacts with the modifier to make it invisible while still performing its effect.

Visible Ink

Oh, changed your mind and want to see the ugly modifier again? Luckily for indecisive you, a bucket of milk is enough to make the modifier visible again.
