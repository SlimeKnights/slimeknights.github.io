Smite

Fear me undead...

For I wield the power of melons infused with gold! Perhaps golden melon armor would make them stay away from me.

Effects:

  • Increases damage to the undead
  • Multiple levels, each level further increases damage
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Swiftstrike
