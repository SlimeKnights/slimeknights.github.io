Sand Casts

Sand casts can also be created in the part builder by placing sand or a blank sand cast in the pattern slot. This is required to create armor plating casts.

Gold Casts

Gold casts are reusable. To make one, simply pour one ingots worth of molten gold over a tool part placed in a casting table. The tool part is used up in the process.
