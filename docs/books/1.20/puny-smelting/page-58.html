Necrotic

Harvest the life of your foes in order to survive longer.

Effects:

  • Critical hits steal life from your enemies to heal you
  • Multiple levels, increases amount of life steal per level
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level

Padded

A little bit of floofhips never hurt anyone.

Effects:

  • Reduces knockback of tool
  • Multiple levels, each level decreases knockback further
  • Requires 1 upgrade slot per level
